GTA V - The ultimate open-world adventure

Grand Theft Auto V is a critically acclaimed action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. Set in the fictional city of Los Santos, the game follows the lives of three protagonists – Michael, Franklin, and Trevor – as they navigate the criminal underworld and carry out a series of heists.

Franklin Clinton

Franklin is a young man who works as a repo man for a car dealership. He is trying to break out of the cycle of poverty and crime that he was born into and dreams of making it big. Franklin is a skilled driver and has a strong sense of loyalty to those he considers friends.

Michael De Santa

Michael is a former criminal who has entered witness protection after a failed heist several years ago. He is a middle-aged man who lives in a wealthy neighborhood with his wife and two children, but he yearns for the excitement of his criminal past. Michael is intelligent, ruthless, and has a quick wit.

Trevor Philips

Trevor is a psychopathic former military pilot who now operates a drug empire in the desert outside of Los Santos. He is impulsive, unpredictable, and prone to violent outbursts. Trevor and Michael have a complicated history, and their relationship is strained throughout the game. Despite his flaws, Trevor has a twisted sense of humor and is often a source of comic relief.
